I'm Iván Andrés

I'm just an Spanish 21-year-old boy who loves doing photos.

I live in Valencia, one of the biggest city in Spain, and also one of the most popular ones. I like doing all kind of photos, but which the ones enjoy the most are the urban ones. I love going out with my camera and look for a cool photo, it doesn't matter where I am.

Now i'm on my Erasmus exchange in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. This city stole my heart in all ways. The city centre is awesome, all the buildings have their own vibe and the streets are beautiful. I also travelled to a lot of places in this country: Turda, Oradea, Bucharest... And in all of them i found something special and completely different to have a photo of.

I'm a photographer who likes to share some of the captures that I took, just to show the world with my point of view, trying to find beauty in the most normal things, for exaple, the street in which you are all days and you know perfectly. There's a cool photo to do everywhere.

Here you can see some of my photos, if you want to see more of my work, you just have to go to the gallery on the top menu!
